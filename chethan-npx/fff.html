About Ivaan

Ivaan narrates the epic journey of Lord Rama, the seventh avatar of Vishnu, revered as the epitome of virtue and honor. This cinematic masterpiece delves into the legendary tale of Rama's exile, his battle against the forces of evil, and the ultimate triumph of good over evil. It is a celebration of courage, sacrifice, and divine grace, bringing timeless values to life on the silver screen.
